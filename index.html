About Spruce and Sweep

At Spruce and Sweep, we understand that a well-kept home and a beautifully maintained outdoor space are essential to your comfort and happiness. Based in Jamaica, we offer top-tier housekeeping and landscaping services designed to transform your living spaces into pristine, serene havens. With a team of dedicated professionals and a commitment to excellence, we take care of the cleaning, organizing, and outdoor care so you can focus on what matters most.

Comprehensive Services for Your Home and Outdoors

Our dual expertise in housekeeping and landscaping ensures that every corner of your home and yard is immaculate. Services include:

Housekeeping Services:

Our housekeeping services are designed to provide a spotless and well-organized living or working environment. We specialize in deep cleaning to eliminate dirt, dust, and grime from every corner, ensuring your space feels fresh and rejuvenated. For ongoing upkeep, our regular maintenance services keep your property in pristine condition, tailored to your schedule and specific needs. Additionally, we offer professional organization solutions to declutter and optimize your spaces, creating a more functional and aesthetically pleasing environment. Whether it's a one-time deep clean or a consistent care plan, we ensure your satisfaction and peace of mind.

Landscaping Services:

Our landscaping services are crafted to enhance the beauty and functionality of your outdoor spaces. We specialize in garden design, creating visually stunning and harmonious layouts that reflect your style and preferences. Our lawn care services ensure lush, healthy grass through regular maintenance, trimming, and nourishment. For a more structured touch, we offer hardscaping solutions, including patios, walkways, retaining walls, and other durable elements to complement your landscape. From concept to completion, our team is dedicated to transforming your outdoor areas into captivating and enjoyable environments tailored to your vision.
